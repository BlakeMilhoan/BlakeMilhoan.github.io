Who We Are

Everyone here at Pacific Trails Resort is dedicated to making your stay an incredible one. Our staff is highly trained and ready to answer any questions you might have during, before, and after your stay. Whether it be Hiking, Kayaking, Bird Watching, or just taking a stroll though the beautiful scenery, we are here to help you make the most of your time with us.
